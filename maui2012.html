Subfactors in Maui 2012 July 16-20, 2012
Program Description
 Subfactors in Maui will be held July 16-20 in Maui, HI. Topics will include subfactors, fusion categories, planar algebras, free probability, and conformal field theory. The schedule of talks is as follows.

Information for participants
 The organizers will arrange for most participants to stay at Maui Sunset in Kihei. There are nearby alternatives for anyone who would prefer to stay elsewhere. Talks will be held at the Maui Beach Hotel and UH Maui College. The relevant airport is Kahului Airport (OGG).
