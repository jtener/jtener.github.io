Subfactors in Tahoe 2012 February 20-27, 2012
Program Description
 Subfactors in Tahoe will be held in South Lake Tahoe, February 20-27, 2012. Topics include subfactors, planar algebras, von Neumann algebras, topology, and mathematical physics.



Schedule
 Talks will be held in the mornings and evenings.
