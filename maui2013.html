Subfactors in Maui 2013 July 15-19, 2013
Program Description
 Subfactors in Maui will be held July 15-19 in Maui, HI. Topics will include subfactors, fusion categories, planar algebras, free probability, and mathematical physics. The schedule of talks is:

Information for participants
 The organizers will arrange for most participants to stay at Maui Sunset in Kihei. There are nearby alternatives for anyone who would prefer to stay elsewhere. Talks will be held at UH Maui College. The relevant airport is Kahului Airport (OGG).
